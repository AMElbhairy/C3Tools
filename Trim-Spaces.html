Overview

The Trim Spaces module allows users to clean up unwanted spaces in text within Excel cells. It offers three options to remove leading, trailing, or all spaces to help ensure data consistency.

Available Trim Options

Button Description
Trim All Spaces Removes all leading and trailing spaces from cells.
Trim Leading Spaces Removes spaces only at the beginning of the text.
Trim Trailing Spaces Removes spaces only at the end of the text.

How to Use

  1. Open your Excel workbook.
  2. Select any range of cells containing text data.
  3. Click one of the Trim Spaces buttons from the ribbon.
  4. When prompted, confirm or select the range of cells to trim spaces from (defaults to your current selection).
  5. The text in the selected range will be cleaned based on the chosen option.

Example

Notes and Limitations

  • Only affects plain text cells; numbers and formulas remain unchanged.
  • Merged cells or protected sheets may not update correctly.
  • Always save a backup before applying mass changes, as Undo is not supported.
